Architectural Configuration

Our model has been overhauled with modern, hardware-friendly transformer components to maximize capacity and convergence rates inside the 2,000,000 parameter constraint.

Weight Tying
lm_head shares weights with the token embedding matrix, freeing ~370,000 parameters to invest in depth and attention width.
Rotary Positional Embeddings (RoPE)
Applied inside SelfAttention to queries and keys, removing absolute positional lookup tables and enabling better length generalization.
RMSNorm & SwiGLU
Replaced LayerNorm with RMSNorm to bypass mean calculations, and substituted GELU with SwiGLU projection gating inside MLP blocks.
Capacity & Dimension Tuning
Configured with 4 Layers, 6 Attention Heads, and 180 Embedding Dimension (head dimension 30), totaling exactly 1,928,340 parameters.
